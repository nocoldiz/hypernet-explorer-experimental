LARD-TASTIC BEEF GRAVY
INGREDIENTS: * 500G BEEF DRIPPING * 4 TBSP LARD * 2 TBSP FLOUR * 1 CUP BEEF STOCK * 1 TSP SALT * BLACK PEPPER
METHOD: 1. HEAT DRIPPING IN PAN UNTIL BUBBLING 2. ADD LARD AND MELT COMPLETELY 3. STIR IN FLOUR TO MAKE ROUX 4. SLOWLY ADD STOCK, STIRRING CONSTANTLY TO AVOID LUMPS 5. SIMMER 10 MINS UNTIL THICK 6. SEASON WITH SALT & PEPPER SERVE WITH ROAST BEEF AND YORKSHIRE PUDDINGS FOR A PROPER SUNDAY DINNER.

GRAN'S BACON FAT COUNTRY GRAVY
INGREDIENTS: * 1 CUP BACON FAT (SAVED FROM BREAKFAST FRYUPS) * 1/2 CUP PLAIN FLOUR * 4 CUPS WHOLE MILK * 1 TSP SALT * 2 TSP BLACK PEPPER
METHOD: 1. HEAT BACON FAT IN CAST IRON SKILLET UNTIL MELTED 2. ADD FLOUR AND COOK 5 MINS STIRRING CONTINUOUSLY 3. SLOWLY WHISK IN MILK 4. BRING TO BOIL THEN REDUCE HEAT 5. SIMMER 10 MINS UNTIL THICK 6. ADD SALT AND LOTS OF PEPPER POUR OVER BISCUITS (P.168) FOR AUTHENTIC SOUTHERN BREAKFAST.
DUCK FAT ONION GRAVY
INGREDIENTS: * 6 TBSP DUCK FAT * 4 LARGE ONIONS, SLICED * 2 TBSP PLAIN FLOUR * 500ML CHICKEN STOCK * 1 TBSP WORCESTER SAUCE * 1 TSP SUGAR * SALT & PEPPER
METHOD: 1. HEAT DUCK FAT IN HEAVY PAN 2. ADD ONIONS AND COOK VERY SLOWLY FOR 30 MINS UNTIL CARAMELISED 3. SPRINKLE FLOUR OVER ONIONS AND STIR WELL 4. GRADUALLY ADD STOCK 5. ADD WORCESTER SAUCE & SUGAR 6. SIMMER 15 MINS 7. SEASON TO TASTE PERFECT WITH BANGERS & MASH OR TOAD IN THE HOLE (P.169).
EMERGENCY INSTANT GRAVY
INGREDIENTS: * 2 TBSP BUTTER * 2 TBSP DRIPPING OR LARD * 2 TBSP FLOUR * 1 STOCK CUBE * 1 CUP BOILING WATER * 1 TSP GRAVY BROWNING
METHOD: 1. MELT BUTTER AND DRIPPING TOGETHER 2. ADD FLOUR AND COOK 2 MINS 3. CRUMBLE IN STOCK CUBE 4. GRADUALLY ADD WATER 5. ADD GRAVY BROWNING 6. STIR UNTIL THICKENED WHEN YOU'VE FORGOTTEN TO MAKE PROPER GRAVY - THIS WILL DO!
