PUBLIC TRANSPORT

The Flesh Arcade is conveniently located near several major transit lines:

  • Cyber Line: Digital District Station (Exit 4) - 2 minute walk
  • Neon Loop: Electronics Plaza Station (Exit 2) - 5 minute walk
  • Metro: Akihabara Station (North Exit) - 7 minute walk
  • Bus: Routes 45, 63, 77 stop directly in front of building

Hours of Operation:

  • Cyber Line: 05:00-01:00 (weekdays), 24 hours (weekends)
  • Neon Loop: 05:30-00:30 (daily)
  • Metro: 05:00-01:00 (daily)
  • Night Buses: Routes N7, N15 operate when trains are not running

WALKING DIRECTIONS

From Digital District Station (Exit 4):

  1. Exit the station via Exit 4 (Cyber Mall side)
  2. Turn right onto Electric Avenue
  3. Walk past the Quantum Arcade (on your left)
  4. Continue for approximately 100 meters
  5. The Flesh Arcade is on your right, identified by the large neon sign

Estimated walking time: 2 minutes

From Electronics Plaza Station (Exit 2):

  1. Exit the station via Exit 2 (Digital Market side)
  2. Turn left and walk to the first major intersection
  3. Turn right onto Virtual Boulevard
  4. At the second intersection, turn left onto Electric Avenue
  5. Walk approximately 150 meters
  6. The Flesh Arcade is on your left, across from CyberByte Store

Estimated walking time: 5 minutes

From Akihabara Station (North Exit):

  1. Exit the station via the North Exit
  2. Follow signs for "Digital District"
  3. Cross the main avenue at the traffic lights
  4. Continue straight for two blocks
  5. Turn right at Neon Street
  6. Take the first left onto Electric Avenue
  7. The Flesh Arcade is approximately 200 meters on your right

Estimated walking time: 7 minutes
