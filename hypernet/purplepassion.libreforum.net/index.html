About Purple Passion Forum

Welcome to the Hypernet's premier destination for purple enthusiasts!

Purple Passion Forum was founded in January 2001 by a group of European purple lovers who wanted to create a dedicated space for discussing everything related to the color purple. From the deepest violets to the softest lavenders, we celebrate every shade and hue.

What we discuss:

  • Purple phenomena in nature (auroras, sunsets, flowers)
  • Purple gemstones and crystal collecting
  • Purple art, design, and decoration
  • Purple foods and beverages
  • Purple fashion and trends
  • Purple photography and digital art
  • Scientific aspects of purple light and color

Forum Rules:

  1. Keep discussions purple-related
  2. Be respectful to all members
  3. No spam or commercial posts
  4. Use appropriate titles for topics
  5. Share your purple photos responsibly
