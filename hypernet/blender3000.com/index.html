About BlendTastic Products Inc.

Founded in 1985, BlendTastic Products Inc. has been the LEADER in kitchen innovation for over a decade!

Our founder, Jim "Smoothie King" Johnson, had a vision: to create blenders that would transform ordinary kitchens into EXTRAORDINARY juice bars and cooking centers!
