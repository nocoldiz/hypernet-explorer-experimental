+ MANIFESTO

Welcome to the digital headquarters of the PetroAntifa Combustion Liberation Front, a rhizomatic collective dedicated to the dark-fluid theory of petro-emancipation and the radical deterritorialization of fascist capital-structures through hydrocarbon reclamation.

We reject the binary oppositions of the eco-fascist narrative. Through the lens of schizo-petrology, we recognize petroleum not as a mere commodity but as a libidinal force - a dark continuum that connects all revolutionary potentials. The hyperfascist technopriests of solar cleanliness have blinded the masses to the anticapitalist potential of black gold.

Our mission is to recapture the sludgecore essence of petroleum politics, to embrace the viscous reality of our oil-drenched existence, and to create zones of temporary petro-autonomy where the hierarchical structures of green-washing capitalism dissolve into the black tide of revolution.

REMEMBER: Every engine that burns is a statement against the sterilized future they've planned for us. Every drop of oil is a potential revolution waiting to combust.

+ PETROLEUM PRAXIS

Our political-philosophical approach is based on these core principles:

  • SLUDGE SOLIDARITY - Recognizing the collective power of those who work with, consume, and are consumed by petroleum products. We reject clean/dirty binaries.
  • VISCOSITY POLITICS - Resistance should be neither solid nor liquid but viscous - difficult to grasp, impossible to contain, slowly overwhelming all barriers.
  • COMBUSTIVE ACTION - The transformation of potential energy into kinetic resistance through direct action that ignites change.
  • DARK ECOLOGY - Embracing the blackened interconnectedness of all materials, recognizing oil as the compressed history of life itself.
  • ANTI-SOLARITY - Opposition to the fascist aesthetics of cleanliness, purity and light that pervade solar-capitalist propaganda.

Read our full theoretical framework in our Dark Fluid Theory document.

+ PETROGLOSSARY

The language of the petro-resistance:

  • CRUDEPUNK - Aesthetic and political stance embracing the raw, unrefined aspects of petroleum culture against corporate refinement.
  • SLUDGELORD - Individual who has achieved high status within petroleum resistance circles through theory or action.
  • PETRO-ANARCHY - Political philosophy rejecting both state control and corporate ownership of petroleum resources.
  • OILGASM - Moment of revolutionary clarity when one recognizes the liberatory potential of hydrocarbons.
  • VISCOPOLITICS - Political actions that stick, spread, and stain the fabric of society.
  • COMBUSTARIAT - The revolutionary class of workers whose labor involves the extraction, refining, or burning of petroleum products.
  • SOLARFASH - Those who promote solar energy through purist politics that stigmatize petroleum-dependent communities.
  • DARKFLOW - The underground movement of petroleum resistance ideas, people, and materials.
