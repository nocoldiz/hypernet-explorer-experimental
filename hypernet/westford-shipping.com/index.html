About Bubba Williams

Howdy! Name's Bubba Williams, born and raised right here in Westford, Texas. Been working with trucks since I was knee-high to a grasshopper - my daddy ran Williams Auto Repair, and I learned to turn a wrench before I could properly tie my shoes!

Started Westford Interstate Shipping back in 1987 with nothing but a used Peterbilt, a dream, and a whole lot of Texas determination. That old truck became "The Beast" after I rebuilt her engine, transmission, and just about everything else. She's been my faithful companion on over 2.8 million miles of American highways.

Professional Credentials

  • CDL Class A - Clean driving record
  • DOT Safety Rating: Excellent
  • Licensed in all 48 contiguous states
  • Hazmat endorsement certified
  • ASE Certified Master Technician
  • Member: Texas Trucking Association
