HOW TO REGISTER

To register for Foundation events:

  • Click the registration link associated with the event
  • Complete the online registration form
  • For fee-based events, submit payment via secure transaction
  • You will receive a confirmation email with details

Registration is typically required for training sessions and workshops to ensure adequate materials and seating. Some public lectures and forums may not require registration but it is recommended.

For questions about registration or events, please contact our Events Coordinator at events@hexagram.corp or call 1-800-INFO-EDU.

HOST AN EVENT

The Archive Foundation can provide speakers and trainers for organizations interested in hosting information safety events. We offer presentations tailored to various audiences including:

  • Schools and educational institutions
  • Community organizations
  • Corporate environments
  • Government agencies
  • Libraries and museums

To inquire about hosting an event, please complete our host request form at least 8 weeks before your proposed date.
