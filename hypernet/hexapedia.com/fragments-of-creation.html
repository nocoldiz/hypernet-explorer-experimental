Fragments of Creation

Last updated: November 3, 2001 | 10 min read

Fragments of Creation are primordial reality-prototypes discarded by The Gods during their initial universe-construction experiments billions of years ago. These ancient blueprint-remnants contain raw creation-algorithms and foundational physics-parameters that predate all known dimensional-structures.

While both exist as reality-debris, Dimensional Scraps are recent byproducts of the 1997 Squishing Event containing compressed matter from collapsed dimensions. Divine Origins

According to Archive Foundation theological-archaeology division records, Fragments of Creation represent abandoned iterations from The Gods' universe-development phase. During this primordial period, divine entities experimented with various reality-architectures before settling on the stable dimensional-framework that eventually became the 92-layer multiverse.

The creation-process appears to have involved iterative prototype-testing, where The Gods would construct experimental reality-bubbles, evaluate their stability-parameters, and then discard unsuccessful configurations. These discarded prototypes became the Fragments of Creationâ€”reality-seeds containing incomplete but potentially powerful creation-algorithms.

"Each Fragment represents a path not taken in universal development. They contain physics-laws that were considered but ultimately rejected by divine consensus. Some fragments show evidence of realities where gravity operated inversely, or where time flowed in multiple simultaneous directions." - Dr. Helena Voss, Divine Archaeology Department, Archive Foundation, 2001

Fundamental Properties

Fragments of Creation exhibit several unique characteristics that distinguish them from all other reality-anomalies:

  • Causality-Immunity: Fragments exist outside normal cause-effect relationships, making them immune to temporal-manipulation and reality-alteration attempts.
  • Physics-Variance: Each fragment contains its own localized physics-constants that may differ radically from baseline reality-parameters.
  • Divine-Signature: Fragments emit detectable creation-frequency patterns that match theoretical models of divine construction-methodology.
  • Expansion-Potential: Under specific conditions, fragments can begin expanding into full reality-matrices, potentially creating new universe-instances.

Classification System

The Mages Guild theological-research division has developed a classification system based on fragment complexity-levels and divine-origin signatures:

Genesis-Class Fragments

The most ancient and complex fragments, containing complete but abandoned universe-blueprints. Genesis-Class fragments often exhibit self-contained ecosystems operating under alien physics-laws. Only 23 Genesis-Class fragments have been documented, and all are maintained under maximum security-containment.

Prototype-Class Fragments

Mid-complexity fragments representing partially-developed reality-concepts. These fragments typically contain experimental physics-systems or incomplete dimensional-structures. Prototype-Class fragments comprise approximately 60% of all documented fragments.

Concept-Class Fragments

The smallest and most numerous fragments, containing basic reality-concepts or fundamental-principles. These fragments often represent individual physics-laws or mathematical-constants that were tested and rejected during the creation-process.

Divine Hazard Warning

Unauthorized interaction with Fragments of Creation may result in reality-displacement, consciousness-divine-merger, or spontaneous universe-creation. All fragment-research requires approval from both the Mages Guild Divine-Research Committee and Archive Foundation Theological-Safety Board.

Containment Protocols

Due to their immense reality-altering potential, Fragments of Creation require specialized containment-methodologies developed jointly by the Mages Guild and Archive Foundation:

Divine-Isolation Chambers

Fragments are housed within reality-isolation chambers constructed using materials extracted from Dimensional Scraps that exhibit null-divine signatures. These chambers prevent fragments from interacting with baseline reality while allowing for controlled observation-protocols.

Continuous Reality-Monitoring

All fragment-sites maintain 24/7 reality-integrity scanning to detect any signs of expansion-activation. Should a fragment begin universe-creation processes, emergency reality-compression protocols can be initiated to abort the expansion-sequence.

Specialized Personnel Requirements

Only researchers who have undergone divine-exposure conditioning and reality-anchor implantation are permitted direct fragment-interaction. Standard personnel lack the cognitive-framework necessary to perceive fragment-properties without suffering consciousness-fragmentation.

Research Applications

Despite containment-challenges, Fragments of Creation offer unprecedented insights into fundamental reality-construction principles:

Alternative Physics Development

By studying the variant physics-laws contained within fragments, researchers have developed new theoretical-frameworks for understanding reality-manipulation. Several breakthrough thaumic-economic principles were derived from fragment-analysis.

Creation-Algorithm Analysis

The Hypercapitalist Collective has invested heavily in fragment-research, seeking to reverse-engineer divine creation-processes for commercial universe-development projects. Their "Reality-Genesis" program aims to create pocket-universes optimized for resource-extraction.

"If we can understand how The Gods built universes, we can build better ones. More efficient ones. Profitable ones." - Internal Memo, Esoteric Heavy Industries Theological-Research Division, 2001

Divine Archaeology

Fragment-study has revealed crucial information about divine construction-methodologies and prehistoric entity-behavior. This research has implications for understanding current divine-politics and predicting future reality-modifications by active deities.

The study of Fragments of Creation continues to challenge fundamental assumptions about reality-construction and divine-capabilities. As research-techniques improve, fragments may provide the key to understanding not just how universes are built, but why specific reality-configurations were chosen over alternatives.
