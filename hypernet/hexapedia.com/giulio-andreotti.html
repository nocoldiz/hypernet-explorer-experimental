Giulio Andreotti

Last updated: December 20, 2001 | 15 min read

Giulio Andreotti (born January 14, 1919) is an Italian politician, former Prime Minister, and confirmed practitioner of multiversal manipulation. Following his acquisition of gravity-based powers through the 1984 Razzi Pact, Andreotti has become one of the most dangerous dimensional architects in recorded history, directly responsible for the retroactive Squishing phenomenon threatening all reality. His transformation from mundane politician to cosmic threat represents the most significant breach of dimensional security protocols since the Archive Foundation's establishment.

Early Life and Political Career

Childhood and Education (1919-1946)

Born in Rome to Filippo Andreotti and Elisabetta Grolimone, Giulio Andreotti displayed no anomalous abilities during his early years. Archive Foundation retrospective analysis of his childhood records reveals consistently normal thaumic-signature readings, indicating he was born entirely without power-potential. This makes his later transformation through artificial enhancement all the more remarkable and dangerous.

Andreotti's education at the Sapienza University of Rome, where he studied law, proceeded along conventional academic trajectories. His thesis on corporate legal structures, completed in 1941, showed no awareness of the dimensional-commerce principles that would later become central to his power base. However, classified Vatican documents from this period mention several meetings between young Andreotti and Cardinal Giuseppe Pizzardo, discussions which remain sealed under papal authority.

His entry into politics in 1946 as a protégé of Alcide De Gasperi appeared entirely mundane, following conventional thaumic-political protocols without any indication of latent power-potential. Early colleagues described him as methodical, calculating, and possessed of an unusual ability to survive political crises—though this was later revealed to be early manifestations of probability-manipulation rather than natural political acumen.

Rise Through the Ranks (1946-1979)

Throughout the 1950s and 1960s, Andreotti's rise through the ranks of the Christian Democratic Party was methodical but unremarkable by post-Y2K standards. He served in various ministerial positions, including Defense (1959-1966), Treasury (1958-1959), and Industry (1966-1968), developing what colleagues described as an uncanny ability to survive political scandals and navigate complex bureaucratic challenges.

What his contemporaries attributed to political cunning was actually the first manifestation of his future dimensional awareness. Internal Mages Guild documents from the 1960s note several instances of "probability distortion events" centered around Andreotti's political activities. These micro-anomalies included:

  • Document Survival: Incriminating papers would mysteriously disappear or become illegible at critical moments
  • Witness Confusion: Key testimony against him would become contradictory or unreliable
  • Timing Manipulation: Crises would resolve themselves through seemingly coincidental events
  • Media Distraction: Larger scandals would emerge to overshadow his involvement in smaller ones

The Pecorelli Investigation (1979)

The 1979 assassination of journalist Mino Pecorelli marked a crucial turning point in Andreotti's dimensional awareness. Pecorelli had been investigating what he called "parallel governance structures," unknowingly documenting evidence of multiversal political interference patterns that would not be formally recognized by anomalous organizations until after the Y2K emergence.

Pecorelli's unpublished notes, recovered by Archive Foundation operatives in 1985, contained detailed descriptions of:

  • Shadow Cabinet Meetings: Government sessions attended by individuals who cast no reflections
  • Temporal Document Anomalies: Official papers dated years before the events they described
  • Dimensional Communications: Phone conversations with voices claiming to represent "alternate Italian governments"
  • Probability Markets: Financial instruments betting on the outcomes of events that hadn't yet been proposed

His death, officially attributed to organized crime connections, was actually carried out by operatives working for entities from adjacent reality-streams. The assassination was performed using a weapon that fired bullets from multiple timelines simultaneously, ensuring that no single reality would bear complete responsibility for the killing—a technique that would later become standard in dimensional warfare.

The Razzi Pact of 1984

First Contact with The Razzi's Hand

In 1984, while serving his fourth term as Prime Minister, Andreotti made contact with a collective of outer-dimensional entities known as The Razzi's Hand. These beings, existing in the conceptual spaces between realities, had been observing his unconscious probability-manipulation abilities and identified him as a suitable candidate for dimensional partnership.

The Razzi's Hand operates as a parasitic collective consciousness that feeds on the collapse of dimensional barriers. Unlike traditional cosmic entities that exist within specific realities, they inhabit the transitional zones between universes, growing stronger as the boundaries between worlds weaken. Their name derives from their primary manifestation: a seven-fingered appendage that appears to "tear" through the fabric of space-time.

Initial contact occurred during a private meeting in Villa San Martino, Andreotti's residence. Archive Foundation reconstruction of the event, based on thaumic-residue analysis, indicates the Razzi entities materialized through a temporary gravitational anomaly created by the convergence of multiple probability-streams that Andreotti had been unconsciously manipulating for decades.

"Power is not held, it is borrowed from those who understand its true source. I have simply negotiated better terms than most." - Giulio Andreotti, private diary entry, November 1984

Terms and Conditions of the Agreement

The Razzi Pact represented one of the most comprehensive human-entity agreements in recorded history. The terms included:

  • Biological Immortality: Complete freedom from aging, disease, and natural death through cellular regeneration enhancement
  • Gravitational Authority: Ability to manipulate local gravity fields, spatial curvature, and mass-energy relationships
  • Probability Dominance: Conscious control over chance events within a 50-kilometer radius
  • Razzi Membership: Full inclusion in the outer-dimensional collective's decision-making processes
  • Dimensional Access Protocols: Ability to perceive, communicate with, and travel between parallel reality-streams
  • Entity Protection: Immunity from attacks by other anomalous organizations and dimensional threats

In exchange, Andreotti agreed to systematically weaken the dimensional barriers separating the third dimension from the fifth dimension—a realm composed entirely of accumulated unconscious thoughts and dreams from all parallel realities. This process required precise manipulation of Italy's political, economic, and social structures to create specific resonance-frequencies in the national consciousness-matrix.

The Implementation Plan

The dimensional weakening project was designed as a decades-long process involving multiple phases:

Phase One (1984-1992): Infrastructure Preparation

  • Establishment of shell corporations with interdimensional investment capabilities
  • Recruitment of key personnel with latent thaumic sensitivity
  • Construction of resonance amplification sites throughout Italy
  • Development of probability-manipulation networks within government bureaucracy

Phase Two (1992-2001): Systematic Destabilization

  • Creation of political crises to generate national anxiety-resonance
  • Economic manipulation to create wealth-disparity stress patterns
  • Media control operations to amplify dimensional frequency disruption
  • Judicial system interference to undermine reality-anchor stability

Phase Three (2001-2012): Reality Breach Preparation

  • Coordination with parallel-universe Andreottis
  • Construction of the dimensional tear apparatus
  • Accumulation of sufficient consciousness-energy for the breach
  • Final integration with The Razzi's Hand collective

Post-Pact Transformation

Physical and Mental Modifications

The enhancement process began immediately after the pact's completion. Andreotti's cellular structure underwent radical modification, with his DNA being rewritten to include sequences derived from Razzi genetic templates. The transformation was gradual to avoid detection by contemporary medical monitoring systems.

Observable changes included:

  • Personal Gravitational Field: Objects within his immediate vicinity experience subtle weight variations
  • Enhanced Temporal Perception: Ability to perceive multiple probable futures simultaneously
  • Dimensional Vision: Capacity to observe events occurring in parallel realities
  • Probability Intuition: Instinctive understanding of statistical manipulation techniques
  • Entity Communion: Direct mental communication with other Razzi collective members

Initial Power Testing Phase

Between 1984 and 1987, Andreotti conducted extensive testing of his new abilities, using Italy's complex political landscape as an experimental environment. Notable demonstrations included:

The Bettino Craxi Experiment (1985)

Using probability manipulation, Andreotti orchestrated the rise and fall of Socialist leader Bettino Craxi, demonstrating his ability to control complex political narratives across multiple timeline branches. The experiment proved that his powers could influence not just immediate events but entire political careers spanning years.

The Aldo Moro Retroactive Investigation (1986)

Andreotti used his dimensional vision capabilities to observe alternate timelines where Aldo Moro had not been killed by the Red Brigades in 1978. This investigation revealed that Moro's death had been orchestrated by entities from a parallel reality where he had successfully exposed interdimensional political interference—making his assassination a multiversal necessity.

The Vatican Resonance Test (1987)

Working with Cardinal Joseph Ratzinger, Andreotti conducted experiments in religious consciousness manipulation, using Catholic prayer networks to amplify his dimensional weakening effects. The test demonstrated that organized spiritual activities could be weaponized to destabilize reality-anchors on a national scale.

The 2009 Wedding Assault

The 2009 Wedding Assault represents one of the most brazen displays of Andreotti's post-enhancement capabilities. During the wedding ceremony of his cousin Teresa Baratheon, Andreotti launched an unprovoked gravitational attack, attempting to crush her using localized gravity manipulation. The assault resulted in Teresa's immediate coma and the complete destruction of the ceremonial venue. Archive Foundation investigators later determined that Teresa possessed latent dimensional-awareness abilities that Andreotti viewed as a potential threat to his operations.

The 2011 Assassination Orders

While Teresa remained in medical care, Andreotti issued termination orders through his network of probability-manipulated assets. The 2011 assassination attempt was thwarted only by intervention from Mages Guild protective services, who had been monitoring the Baratheon family due to their anomalous heritage. This incident marked the first direct conflict between Andreotti and major anomalous organizations.

The 2021 Final Confrontation

The 2021 confrontation occurred following Teresa's recovery and attempted exposure of Andreotti's multiversal activities. Despite years of combat training and protective enchantments provided by Guild allies, she proved completely outmatched against Andreotti's fully realized gravitational powers. The encounter concluded with Teresa being marked with what Archive Foundation classifications term a "Sacrifice Sigil"—a Razzi-derived curse that designates the bearer for dimensional consumption—before being buried alive in a probability-locked tomb that exists simultaneously across multiple realities.

Multiversal Operations

Discovery of Parallel Andreottis

Archive Foundation researchers have confirmed the existence of at least forty-seven versions of Giulio Andreotti across parallel realities, each having made similar pacts with their respective Razzi's Hand entities. These alternate Andreottis have succeeded or failed in their dimensional weakening projects at different temporal coordinates, creating a complex web of multiversal interference patterns.

Analysis of the known variants reveals several distinct categories:

  • Successful Variants (7 confirmed): Andreottis who completed their dimensional breach projects and achieved full Razzi integration
  • Failed Variants (23 confirmed): Versions terminated by their reality's anomalous organizations before project completion
  • Ongoing Variants (12 confirmed): Currently active Andreottis in various stages of their dimensional manipulation projects
  • Corrupted Variants (5 confirmed): Andreottis whose pacts resulted in uncontrolled mutations and reality distortions

The Multiversal Coordination Network

What makes our dimension's Andreotti particularly dangerous is his unique ability to coordinate with his parallel selves. Using gravitational-bridge technology developed through Razzi knowledge, he established communication channels with other successful variants, enabling synchronized attacks on dimensional stability across multiple realities.

The coordination network operates through:

  • Gravitational Communication Bridges: Quantum-entangled gravitational waves carrying information between realities
  • Consciousness Overlap Protocols: Temporary mental fusion allowing shared strategic planning
  • Probability Synchronization: Coordinated manipulation of chance events across multiple timelines
  • Interdimensional Resource Sharing: Exchange of personnel, equipment, and strategic assets between realities

Dimensional Warfare Techniques

Reality Weakening Methodologies

Andreotti's methods of dimensional weakening combined his political expertise with Razzi-enhanced abilities, creating a sophisticated approach to reality manipulation that operated simultaneously on multiple levels of existence.

Economic Warfare Protocols

Economic manipulation served as Andreotti's primary vector for dimensional destabilization. His methods included:

  • Currency Distortion Fields: Using probability manipulation to create artificial exchange rate fluctuations that generate thaumic instabilities in national economic consciousness
  • Sovereign Debt Spiral Engineering: Orchestrating national debt crises that create anxiety-resonance patterns in the collective unconscious
  • Inflation Weaponization: Manipulating price stability to undermine citizen confidence in reality-consensus
  • Market Volatility Amplification: Creating stock market chaos that weakens the financial system's reality-anchor properties

Political Resonance Operations

Political manipulation focused on creating governmental instability that would generate reality-stress patterns:

  • Coalition Collapse Engineering: Using gravitational influence to create personality conflicts between political leaders
  • Scandal Amplification Matrices: Probability manipulation to ensure maximum damage from political controversies
  • Referendum Manipulation: Influencing public votes to create constitutional crises and legal reality paradoxes
  • Bureaucratic Entropy Generation: Creating administrative chaos to destabilize local reality-anchors through procedural confusion

Information Warfare and Consciousness Manipulation

Andreotti recognized early that modern media networks could be weaponized for dimensional manipulation. His information operations included:

  • Narrative Fragmentation Campaigns: Simultaneously promoting contradictory versions of events to weaken consensus reality
  • Truth Relativity Amplification: Encouraging epistemological uncertainty to destabilize objective reality anchors
  • Collective Attention Harvesting: Using media spectacles to redirect national consciousness energy toward dimensional weak points
  • Historical Memory Modification: Subtle alterations to documented history that create temporal paradox stress

The Great Reality Breach

Final Preparation Phase (2010-2012)

By 2010, Andreotti had successfully weakened Italy's dimensional barriers to critical levels. The final phase required precise coordination with his parallel selves and the construction of a massive reality-tearing apparatus hidden beneath Rome's ancient structures.

The dimensional breach apparatus, known as the "Capitoline Resonator," was constructed using:

  • Ancient Foundation Stones: Repurposed Roman infrastructure enhanced with Razzi metallurgy
  • Consciousness Batteries: Accumulated psychic energy from decades of political manipulation
  • Gravitational Focusers: Andreotti's enhanced abilities channeled through technological amplifiers
  • Multiversal Coordinate Arrays: Targeting systems for precise dimensional tear placement

The Breach Event (December 21, 2012)

On December 21, 2012, at precisely 11:11 GMT, Andreotti achieved full membership in The Razzi's Hand collective, completing his dimensional manipulation project. The resulting reality-breach created what Archive Foundation classifications term a "howling void"—a permanent tear in the fabric of existence that extends beyond simple dimensional boundaries into the fundamental structure of causality itself.

The breach process involved:

  1. Multiversal Synchronization: All seven successful Andreottis activated their dimensional apparatus simultaneously
  2. Barrier Collapse Cascade: Sequential failure of dimensional walls starting with the third-fifth dimension boundary
  3. Consciousness Flood: Massive influx of fifth-dimensional dream-matter into base reality
  4. Razzi Integration: Andreotti's consciousness merging with the collective outer-dimensional entity
  5. Reality Rewrite Initialization: Activation of retroactive causality modification protocols

Immediate Effects and Consequences

The reality breach triggered the retroactive Squishing phenomenon, a cascading failure of dimensional stability that operates backwards through time. Unlike the original 1997 Squishing event, this new phenomenon actively rewrites historical causality, threatening not just dimensional collapse but complete erasure of all parallel realities.

Observable effects include:

  • Temporal Anomalies: Events occurring before their causes, creating causal paradox zones
  • Reality Fluctuations: Spontaneous changes in physical laws within breach-affected areas
  • Dimensional Bleeding: Objects and entities from parallel realities spontaneously manifesting in base dimension
  • Consciousness Fragmentation: Individual awareness splitting across multiple timeline variants
  • Memory Instability: Retroactive changes to personal and collective historical records
  • Gravitational Storms: Localized space-time distortions centered around major population centers

Current threat assessments indicate that the breach is expanding at an exponential rate. The Mages Guild estimates complete multiversal collapse within 50-75 years if no intervention occurs. Traditional containment protocols prove ineffective against retroactive causality manipulation, as any successful containment effort can be prevented by altering the past conditions that led to its implementation.

The Council of Seven

Formation and Structure

Following the success of our dimension's reality breach, seven successful versions of Giulio Andreotti from different parallel realities have gathered in what Archive Foundation deep reconnaissance describes as a "nameless palace" existing in the conceptual space outside all multiverses. This Council of Seven represents the most significant existential threat in recorded history, combining the political cunning of history's most successful manipulator with the cosmic power of fully realized Razzi integration.

The nameless palace exists in a state of architectural impossibility, simultaneously incorporating elements from all seven realities while belonging to none. Reconnaissance reports describe:

  • Non-Euclidean Architecture: Corridors that lead to themselves, rooms larger than the buildings containing them
  • Temporal Conference Chambers: Meeting spaces where past, present, and future discussions occur simultaneously
  • Probability Gardens: Landscaping that displays all possible outcomes of current multiversal events
  • Memory Libraries: Archives containing the complete history of every reality the Council has influenced
  • The Gravity Throne: A seat of power that exists at the gravitational center of the multiversal complex

Council Membership and Hierarchy

Each of the seven Andreottis brings unique expertise and dimensional resources to the collective:

  • Prime Andreotti (Our Reality): The original architect of the reality breach, specializing in retroactive causality manipulation
  • Economic Andreotti (Reality-B): Master of interdimensional financial systems and market probability control
  • Military Andreotti (Reality-C): Expert in dimensional warfare tactics and consciousness-based weapons systems
  • Temporal Andreotti (Reality-D): Specialist in timeline manipulation and paradox resolution
  • Spiritual Andreotti (Reality-E): Controller of religious consciousness networks across multiple faith systems
  • Scientific Andreotti (Reality-F): Developer of reality-modification technologies and dimensional physics applications
  • Shadow Andreotti (Reality-G): Intelligence operations coordinator and information warfare specialist

Current Projects and Objectives

Intelligence gathered by Archive Foundation deep-cover operatives suggests that the Council's ambitions extend far beyond simple dimensional manipulation. Their current projects appear focused on what they term "Universal Governance Protocol"—a comprehensive restructuring of existence itself.

Major initiatives include:

  • Universal Reality Rewriting: Complete restructuring of fundamental physical laws across all dimensional boundaries
  • Consciousness Harvesting Operations: Systematic extraction and concentration of sapient awareness from all realities for power amplification
  • Temporal Sovereignty Establishment: Installing direct control over causality itself across all known timelines
  • Conceptual Dominance Protocol: Becoming the fundamental organizing principle of existence by replacing natural laws with Council directives
  • Entity Integration Program: Absorbing other cosmic entities and dimensional powers to expand Council capabilities

Recruitment and Asset Development

The Council maintains extensive recruitment networks focused on identifying individuals with latent reality-manipulation potential. Recruitment criteria include:

Containment Efforts

Archive Foundation Response Protocols

The Archive Foundation has classified the Andreotti Council as a Class-Omega multiversal threat, triggering unprecedented emergency response protocols. Current containment efforts focus on:

  • Reality Anchoring Operations: Installation of dimensional stabilization devices at key geographic nodes
  • Counter-Recruitment Campaigns: Identification and protection of potential Council assets before recruitment
  • Information Warfare Defense: Counter-propaganda operations to maintain consensus reality stability
  • Interdimensional Monitoring Networks: Early warning systems for Council dimensional incursion attempts

Mages Guild Collaborative Efforts

The Mages Guild has initiated "Project Restoration," a comprehensive magical working designed to reinforce dimensional barriers and create Council-resistant reality zones. The project involves:

  • Ward Networks: Magical barrier systems designed to prevent Council probability manipulation
  • Consciousness Shielding: Protective spells to prevent mind control and awareness harvesting
  • Temporal Stabilization Rituals: Magical workings to prevent retroactive history modification
  • Entity Banishment Protocols: Specialized techniques for severing Razzi Hand connections

International Cooperation Efforts

Major world governments have been briefed on the Andreotti threat through carefully managed disclosure protocols. International cooperation efforts include:

  • Political Monitoring Systems: Joint surveillance of government officials for signs of Council influence
  • Economic Stability Protocols: Coordinated financial policies to prevent reality-destabilizing market manipulation
  • Intelligence Sharing Networks: Secure communication systems for anomalous threat information exchange
  • Crisis Response Coordination: Joint protocols for responding to dimensional incursion events

Current Limitations and Challenges

Containment efforts face unprecedented challenges due to the Council's unique capabilities:

  • Retroactive Interference: Council ability to prevent containment by altering past events that led to protective measures
  • Multiversal Resource Base: Access to unlimited personnel and materials from parallel realities
  • Probability Dominance: Council manipulation of chance events to ensure favorable outcomes
  • Deep Infiltration Networks: Unknown extent of Council influence within protective organizations
  • Conceptual Warfare Capabilities: Council ability to attack the fundamental concepts underlying containment efforts

All major anomalous organizations have issued joint warnings regarding any attempts to establish direct contact with the Council of Seven. Direct observation or communication attempts result in immediate conceptual contamination, probability cascade failure, and potential recruitment into their expanding influence network. Even indirect study of Council operations requires Level-5 consciousness shielding and mandatory memory modification upon project completion.
