Dimensional Scraps

Last updated: October 22, 2001 | 8 min read

Dimensional Scraps are fragmented reality-clusters that remain as byproducts of the 1997 Squishing Event. These fragments of the original 92 dimensions float within the Abyss-Stream, containing compressed geo-temporal data and resource-matrices from collapsed universe-instances.

Disambiguation Note

Dimensional Scraps are often confused with the Fragments of Creation. While both exist as reality-debris, Fragments of Creation are ancient prototype-realities discarded by The Gods billions of years ago during their initial universe-construction experiments. Dimensional Scraps, conversely, are recent remnants from the 1997 reality-compression event.

Formation Process

During the catastrophic compression-event that reduced 92 dimensional layers into current reality-configuration, incomplete merger-algorithms created fault-lines in the dimension-welding process. These imperfections resulted in pocket-clusters of unmergeable reality-data being ejected into the Abyss-Stream.

The Archive Foundation has documented that each scrap contains compressed temporal-loops, matter-blueprints, and consciousness-fragments from its origin dimension. The compression ratio typically ranges from 1:10,000 to 1:50,000, meaning entire continents can be stored within scrap-volumes no larger than a city block.

Extraction Warning

Unauthorized scrap-mining is prohibited under Post-Squishing Dimensional Accords. Violation may result in reality-displacement penalties or consciousness-fragmenting.

Scrap Classification System

The Mages Guild has established a standardized taxonomy for dimensional scraps based on content-density and extraction-viability:

  • Alpha-Class Scraps: Contain intact ecosystem-data and functional technology-matrices. Extremely valuable for bio-resource extraction.
  • Beta-Class Scraps: Fragmented civilization-remnants with extractable knowledge-databases and cultural-artifacts.
  • Gamma-Class Scraps: Raw matter-composites suitable for material-synthesis and energy-conversion processes.
  • Omega-Class Scraps: Hazardous fragments containing unstable temporal-loops or consciousness-traps. Designated for quarantine-containment.

Current estimates suggest over 847,000 scraps exist within accessible Abyss-Stream sectors, though only 12% have been properly catalogued by dimensional-survey teams.

Resource Extraction

The Hypercapitalist Collective has pioneered extraction-technologies that can decompress dimensional scraps while maintaining data-integrity. Their patented "Reality-Siphon" systems can process Alpha-Class scraps to yield:

  • Bio-resources from extinct ecosystems
  • Technology-blueprints from advanced civilizations
  • Exotic matter-compounds impossible to synthesize in current reality
  • Temporal-energy stored in collapsed time-loops
"Each dimensional scrap represents an entire universe's worth of untapped market-potential. Our extraction-algorithms can convert dead realities into profit-streams that fuel the next phase of hyper-development." - CEO Statement, Esoteric Heavy Industries, 2001

Navigation Hazards

Scrap-fields present numerous dangers to exploration-teams and extraction-operations:

  • Temporal-Shear Zones: Areas where time-flow becomes unpredictable, potentially aging explorers decades in moments.
  • Memory-Storm Events: Compressed consciousness-data creates psychic interference that can overwrite personal memories.
  • Gravity-Well Distortions: Collapsed matter creates localized physics-anomalies that can trap exploration-vessels.
  • Shadow-Entity Manifestations: Fragments of consciousness from destroyed dimensions may coalesce into hostile pseudo-beings.

Archive Foundation records indicate a 23% casualty rate among authorized scrap-exploration teams, with "reality-displacement syndrome" being the leading cause of permanent personnel loss.

Commercial Applications

Despite extraction-hazards, dimensional scraps have become a cornerstone of the post-Squishing economy. Major applications include:

Manufacturing Enhancement

Exotic materials extracted from scraps have revolutionized production-capabilities. Esoteric Heavy Industries uses scrap-derived compounds to create products that bypass the Rule of 80 limitations, maintaining functionality during time-loop resets.

Information Archaeology

Beta-Class scraps containing civilization-remnants provide invaluable data-resources. Cultural-analysis algorithms can extract artistic-patterns, scientific-principles, and social-structures from collapsed societies, feeding innovation-pipelines across multiple industries.

Energy Generation

Temporal-energy harvesting from compressed time-loops has become a major power-source for dimension-bridging operations. The energy-density of properly processed Gamma-Class scraps exceeds traditional fusion-reactors by orders of magnitude.

Current projections suggest that scrap-based industries will comprise 40% of the hyper-economy by 2005, assuming extraction-technologies continue advancing at current rates.
