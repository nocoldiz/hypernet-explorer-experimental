The Squishing

Last updated: March 8, 2001 | 8 min read

The Squishing (also known as the Dimensional Compression Event or DCE-97) was a multiversal catastrophe that occurred in 1997, resulting in the violent compression of 92 parallel dimensions into a single, unstable reality-matrix. This event fundamentally altered the structure of existence and precipitated the emergence of post-dimensional physics that govern our current reality.

Event Details
Date: September 15, 1997
Duration: 72 minutes
Dimensions Affected: 92 parallel realities
Estimated Casualties: Incalculable
Known Survivors: Earth-Prime population

Event Overview

The Squishing represents the most catastrophic reality-restructuring event in recorded history. Unlike previous dimensional incidents that affected localized space-time sectors, the DCE-97 simultaneously compressed 92 distinct dimensional planes into a single, chaotically overlapping reality-framework. The event's designation comes from Archive Foundation researcher Dr. Helena Voss, who described the phenomenon as "watching the multiverse get squished like an accordion."

Early detection came through dimensional-monitoring stations operated by the Mages Guild, which registered massive flux-distortions beginning at 14:32 GMT. Within minutes, reality-coherence readings across all monitored dimensions began showing critical instability patterns previously thought impossible.

Causal Factors

While the exact trigger remains classified by the Archive Foundation, declassified documents suggest multiple contributing factors:

Hyper-Dimensional Economics

The Hypercapitalist Collective had been conducting aggressive reality-arbitrage operations across multiple dimensions, exploiting temporal-economic disparities to generate massive profit-streams. Their dimensional market-manipulation may have destabilized the fundamental balance-sheets governing multiversal stability.

Thaumic Overextraction

Several dimensions had reached critical thaumic-depletion levels due to intensive magical mining operations. The sudden collapse of magic-based economies in at least 17 dimensions created cascading reality-defaults that propagated through dimensional boundary-layers.

Prototype Technology

Unconfirmed reports suggest Esoteric Heavy Industries was testing experimental dimensional-compression technologies for corporate expansion purposes. Their prototype reality-consolidation engines may have malfunctioned during the September 15th activation sequence.

Compression Process

The Squishing occurred in three distinct phases, each lasting approximately 24 minutes:

Phase One: Dimensional Destabilization

Reality-barriers between dimensions began experiencing critical failure-cascades. Dimensional-monitoring equipment registered impossible readings as separate universes started experiencing mutual contamination-events. Physical laws began fluctuating as competing reality-rulesets attempted to assert dominance over the same space-time coordinates.

Phase Two: Compression Initiation

The actual compression began as dimensional boundaries collapsed entirely. Ninety-two distinct realities were forcibly compressed into overlapping layers, creating chaotic interference-patterns in local space-time. Survivors report experiencing simultaneous memories from multiple dimensional variants of themselves during this phase.

Phase Three: Reality Consolidation

The final phase involved the violent merger of all dimensional matter, energy, and information into a single, extremely unstable reality-matrix. This process created the fundamental irregularities that characterize post-Squishing physics, including the Rule of 80 and various temporal-anomaly zones.

Post-Squishing Reality

The immediate aftermath of the Squishing created a reality-framework operating under entirely new physical principles. The compression of 92 dimensions into a single space resulted in:

  • Temporal Loop Zones: Areas where compressed time-streams create repetitive chronological cycles
  • Dimensional Scraps: Fragments of destroyed dimensions that exist as pocket-realities within our current space-time
  • Unstable Physics: Regions where multiple dimensional physics-packages conflict, creating unpredictable phenomena
  • Dimensional Refugees: Beings from destroyed dimensions who survived the compression process

Perhaps most significantly, the Squishing created the Encompassing Road, a trans-dimensional highway system that connects the scattered remains of the compressed realities. This road network has become crucial for navigation in post-Squishing reality.

Dimensional Survivors

Not all beings from the compressed dimensions were destroyed. Several categories of survivors have been documented:

The Verden Goblins

Originally from the Green Fields dimension, these technologically advanced beings had voluntarily regressed to primitive living before the Squishing. Their rejection of high technology may have protected them from the worst effects of dimensional compression. They now exist as one of several goblin populations in our reality.

Collective Operatives

Various members of the Hypercapitalist Collective survived due to their bio-modifications and speed-enhancement technologies. Their ability to maintain velocities above 80 km/h protected them from the temporal-compression effects.

Unknown Entities

Numerous unclassified beings from unknown dimensions have been detected throughout post-Squishing reality. The Archive Foundation continues cataloging these entities, though many exhibit properties that challenge conventional classification systems.

"The Squishing didn't just compress space and time—it compressed possibility itself. We're living in a reality where 92 different universes' worth of 'what could be' are all trying to exist simultaneously." - Dr. Helena Voss, Archive Foundation Dimensional Research Division, 2000
