Our History

Infernal Tours was founded in 1888 by the eccentric Victorian explorer Sir Alistair Blackwood after his accidental descent into Hell during a spelunking expedition in Romania. Rather than being traumatized by the experience, Sir Alistair saw a business opportunity.

"Why should eternal damnation be limited to the damned?" he famously wrote in his memoir, "A Gentleman's Guide to the Underworld." "Surely the living deserve the educational opportunity to witness divine justice firsthand!"

After extensive negotiations with various demons, devils, and Buddhist hell guardians, Sir Alistair secured exclusive touring rights to both Western and Eastern hell realms. The first official tour departed on Halloween 1888 with a group of adventurous London society members.
