üìö Our History

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Erik Stupidity-Johansson (himself a 2003 posthumous winner), the Valhalla Awards celebrate the intersection of heroism and idiocy. Unlike other awards that mock stupidity, we honor those who died doing something genuinely noble in the most spectacularly dumb way possible.

üß† Our Philosophy

We believe that true heroism isn't about being smart - it's about doing the right thing regardless of personal cost. Our winners prove that you can save the world AND be an complete idiot at the same time. They died as they lived: heroically stupid.

üéØ Our Mission

To document and celebrate those brave souls who made the ultimate sacrifice while displaying the ultimate lack of common sense. Every winner has genuinely made the world a better place through their death, even if their methods were questionable.
