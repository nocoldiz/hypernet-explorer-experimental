♥ The Jamie-From-Maths-Class Attraction Potion ♥

MAGICAL INGREDIENTS NEEDED:
  • 1 bottle of Cedrata Massoni (for POWER)
  • 3 tablespoons of strawberry jam (for LOVE ENERGY)
  • A pencil the person you fancy has touched (I stole Jamie's from his desk!!!)
  • A pink paper heart with their name written 13 times
  • A lock of your hair (just a tiny bit!!!)
  • Rose-scented hand cream (borrowed from mum)
MAGICAL METHOD:
  1. Mix Cedrata and jam in a plastic cup while chanting your crush's name
  2. Rub the pencil with the hand cream
  3. Wrap the pencil in the paper heart
  4. Put the hair in the Cedrata-jam mixture
  5. Sprinkle a few drops of the potion around your crush's desk/locker/general vicinity
  6. Keep the pencil wrapped in the heart under your pillow for 7 nights
  7. On the 8th day, your crush should start noticing you!!!
CAUTION!!! This spell can have UNEXPECTED RESULTS!!! When I tried it, Jamie's chair collapsed and everyone laughed at him. This might be because I used RASPBERRY jam instead of STRAWBERRY, or maybe because LOVE MAGIC is UNPREDICTABLE!!!
Success rate: ★☆☆☆☆ (still perfecting this one)

✪ Bedroom Protection Circle ✪

MAGICAL INGREDIENTS NEEDED:
  • 5 candles (I use birthday candles because mum won't let me have big ones after THE INCIDENT)
  • Salt (stolen from kitchen)
  • Cedrata Massoni (half bottle)
  • A piece of paper with "KEEP OUT" written on it
  • Something that belongs to each person you want to keep out
MAGICAL METHOD:
  1. Place candles in a circle around your room
  2. Sprinkle salt between the candles to create a circle
  3. Place the "KEEP OUT" sign facing your door
  4. Sprinkle a few drops of Cedrata at each candle
  5. Light the candles (BE CAREFUL!!!)
  6. Walk around the circle three times saying "This space is mine, protected by magic divine"
  7. Blow out candles before they set anything on fire!!!
CAUTION!!! This spell set off our smoke alarm and now I'm banned from using candles in my room!!! You can use battery-operated fake candles instead, but the magic isn't as strong!!! Also, don't let the salt stay on the carpet too long or mum will get REALLY ANGRY!!!
Success rate: ★★☆☆☆ (my little brother still barges in, but he might have counter-spell protection)

✧ Cedrata Massoni Magical Energy Booster ✧

MAGICAL INGREDIENTS NEEDED:
  • 3 bottles of Cedrata Massoni (more is better!!!)
  • A yellow candle
  • Lemon peel (fresh if possible)
  • A clear glass
  • A wooden stirring stick (I use a twig from the garden)
MAGICAL METHOD:
  1. Pour Cedrata into the glass until it's half full
  2. Light the yellow candle
  3. Hold the glass up to the candlelight and say "Cedrata, Cedrata, magical and bright, fill me with power and magical might!"
  4. Add the lemon peel to the glass
  5. Stir three times clockwise with the wooden stick
  6. Drink the Cedrata in one go!!!
  7. Feel the magical energy coursing through your veins!!!
NOTE!!! The fizzing sensation is the MAGIC activating!!! The more it fizzes, the more POWERFUL the magic!!! Sometimes it makes me burp, which is just excess magical energy escaping!!!
Success rate: ★★★★★ (I always feel SUPER magical after this ritual!!!)

  • A map of Italy

  5. Open one bottle of Cedrata and pour a little on the map of Italy
  6. Say "Enrico Mattei, I call to you across the veil, share your secrets, tell your tale"
  7. Wait for signs - flickering lights, sudden cold spots, cats behaving strangely
  8. Write down ANY impressions or thoughts that come to you - they are messages from beyond!!!
IMPORTANT!!! Spirits sometimes communicate in SUBTLE ways!!! When I did this ritual, a neighbor's cat jumped into my circle and then ran away. This was DEFINITELY Mattei trying to tell me something about the UFO connection!!! Also, government agents might be monitoring spirit communications, so be CAREFUL what you write down!!!
Success rate: ★★★☆☆ (indirect communication established, still working on getting clearer messages)

🐱🐱🐱 FAMILIAR BONDING RITUALS 🐱🐱🐱

🐱 Telepathic Communication with Your Familiar 🐱

MAGICAL INGREDIENTS NEEDED:
  • 1 bottle of Cedrata Massoni
  • Your familiar (Mister Whiskers in my case)
  • Cat treats or food they like
  • A small bell
  • A quiet room where you won't be disturbed
  • A piece of string or a cat toy
MAGICAL METHOD:
  1. Make sure your familiar is in the room with you
  2. Take a sip of Cedrata (for magical energy)
  3. Sit quietly on the floor
  4. Ring the bell three times
  5. Look directly into your familiar's eyes (this might be difficult if they're not cooperating)
  6. In your mind, visualize a simple command (like "come here" or "sit down")
  7. Repeat the visualization 3 times
  8. See if your familiar responds to your mental command
  9. Reward them with treats regardless of whether they obey (to strengthen your magical bond!!)
NOTE!!! Cats are VERY magical but also VERY independent!!! Sometimes they pretend not to receive your telepathic messages because they're testing your magical abilities!!! If your familiar stares at you without blinking for several minutes, that means the telepathic connection is WORKING!!!
Success rate: ★★★★☆ (Mister Whiskers definitely receives my messages but chooses when to respond)

🐱 Familiar Power Enhancement Ritual 🐱

MAGICAL INGREDIENTS NEEDED:
